Short Bio

Techno-psychological & behavioural expertise

As a techno-psychological and behavioural expert specialising in Human–Computer Interaction (HCI), my career is driven by a deep curiosity about the dynamic interplay between technology and human behaviour. This passion spans digital marketing, immersive gaming, e-sports, education, and AI-mediated communication. My research integrates psychological and behavioural experimentation with advanced simulation methodologies to examine user engagement, information diffusion, AI-driven persuasion, and collective performance metrics, with a strong emphasis on equality, inclusivity, and well-being.

Since joining Loughborough University, my work has gained international recognition, with a significant surge since 2022. I have pioneered an interdisciplinary research area bridging neuroscience and informatics, exploring cognitive and emotional responses to AI tools. My publications in high-impact journals across marketing, information management, and HCI demonstrate my commitment to addressing complex challenges at the intersection of technology, psychology, and society.

Beyond research, I am passionate about developing future leaders and innovators. I have successfully mentored a growing cohort of doctoral and postgraduate students, many of whom have secured academic positions in the UK and China. My leadership extends within and beyond the University: as Chief Programme Director and Programme Director for MSc Digital Marketing, I have shaped curricula that integrate creative media, neuroscience/behavioural science, HCI, and generative AI, preparing graduates for the evolving digital economy.

I have led international research projects funded by EPSRC–AISI, DsTL, GRF, and NSFC, advancing global conversations on AI ethics, neurodiversity, and sustainable innovation. Looking ahead, my aspiration is to lead interdisciplinary, collaborative, and sustainable initiatives that connect technology with human-centric solutions, promoting well-being, equity, and societal harmony.
